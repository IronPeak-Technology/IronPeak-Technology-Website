Terms & Billing

Terms & Billing

Transparent billing with no hidden fees or surprises across all service tiers.

Billing

All services billed monthly. Invoices issued on the 1st of each month for all tiers.

Payment

Net 15 days. Accepted: ACH, check, credit card.

Hourly Billing

Essentials tier support billed in 15-minute increments rounded up at $120/hour.

Setup Fee

One-time $100 per device setup fee for new deployments (all tiers).

Contracts

Month-to-month available. Annual contracts receive a 10% discount.

Referrals

Earn a 5% discount per referred customer who signs up. Stackable.

Support Hours

Mon – Fri, 8 AM – 5 PM ET. After-hours emergency support available.

Server Coverage

Servers not included in Standard/Compliance Complete user plans. Available as separate monthly agreements.

Cancellation

30-day notice required for service changes or cancellation on any service tier.
